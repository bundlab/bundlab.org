# **App Name**: Bundlab.org Website

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Visually engaging introduction highlighting Bundlab's mission with Nigeria-first, global-ready messaging.
- Services Showcase: Detailed presentation of Bundlab's service offerings with clear descriptions and calls to action.
- Team Introduction: Display team member profiles with bios and relevant experience.
- Contact Form: A user-friendly contact form for inquiries and feedback.
- Blog Section: A feed from Bundlab's blog to showcase company updates, thoughts and industry insights.
- AI-Powered Content Suggestion Tool: Suggest blog content or other marketing material to the marketing team to promote Bundlab services, with options for tone and topic adjustments, making use of provided branding guidelines.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Nigerian Green (#008751) to reflect the national identity and growth.
- Background color: Light, desaturated green (#E5F8EF) to create a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: Nigerian White (#FFFFFF) for contrast and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for a modern, machined, objective, neutral look.
- Use modern, minimalist icons that align with the technology and innovation focus of Bundlab.
- Implement a clean, grid-based layout for easy navigation and content consumption.
- Use subtle animations on the hero section and other interactive elements to enhance user experience.
